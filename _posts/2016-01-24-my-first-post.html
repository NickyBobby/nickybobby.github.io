--- title: My First Time Bouldering layout: post date: 2016-01-24 ---

Today I tried bouldering for the first time, and not to brag or anything but I'm kind of amazing at it. Even Kimi (a classmate of mine) said she has never seen anyone with such raw natural talent on the boulder field before. All jokes aside it was pretty amazing, and one hell of a work out. I can't wait to do it again. The one thing that I struggled with was the inverted walls. I feel like my weight and gravity are working against me in those situations. Definitely something I will be working on in the future, and I will conquer it one day!
